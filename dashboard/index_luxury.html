Research Findings

Key Insights

Significant discoveries from our comprehensive seven-model evaluation of EUR/USD forecasting approaches.

01

Hybrid Superiority

The Complete ARIMA-GARCH-LSTM Hybrid achieved 55.08% directional accuracy, significantly outperforming pure ARIMA (0%) and ARIMA-LSTM (36.20%). This validates that combining classical econometrics with deep learning creates substantial forecasting advantages.

02

Residual Learning

ARIMA-LSTM's 36.20% improvement demonstrates that neural networks excel at learning correction patterns from classical model residuals, effectively capturing non-linear relationships that traditional time series methods cannot model.

03

Volatility Modeling

ARIMA-GARCH's 95.46% prediction interval coverage confirms GARCH's effectiveness in FOREX volatility forecasting. The 0.9955 persistence coefficient validates strong volatility clustering in EUR/USD currency movements.

04

Generalization Challenge

Most models exhibited overfitting (65% train → 0-36% test accuracy), except the Complete Hybrid (66% → 55%). This highlights the critical importance of early stopping, dropout regularization, and ensemble approaches for FOREX prediction.

05

Deep Learning Value

Models incorporating LSTM consistently outperformed pure classical approaches across all metrics. Non-linear pattern recognition capabilities prove essential for capturing complex temporal dependencies in high-frequency currency data.

06

Three-Stage Architecture

The Complete Hybrid's success stems from its balanced integration: ARIMA trends (65% contribution), GARCH volatility (18%), and LSTM corrections (17%) work synergistically to achieve superior forecasting performance.
